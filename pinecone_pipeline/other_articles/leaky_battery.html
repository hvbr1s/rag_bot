If you encounter a situation where the battery in your Ledger Nano X device becomes leaky or is damaged due to overheating, please exercise caution. Safely dispose of your device in a designated recycling container. When handling the device, it is recommended to wear gloves for protection. Additionally, consider wearing protective glasses for added safety. Do not dispose of the device in a regular waste bin inside your home. To arrange for a replacement of the damaged device, please open an email ticket with our support team. We will guide you through the process of obtaining a new device.
