If your Ledger Nano X or Nano S Plus device is experiencing hardware issues (such as a malfunctioning button or buttons or screen) and it's still covered by warranty, you can initiate an automated replacement request at https://my-order.ledger.com/. For those with a Ledger Nano S experiencing hardware issues, please reach out to a member of our Support team for assistance. For additional information and guidance, please visit https://support.ledger.com/hc/en-us/articles/10265554529053?support=true.
